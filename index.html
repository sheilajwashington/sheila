About Sheila

Marketing built by someone who actually reads the book.

Sheila J. Washington has spent years championing authors long before anyone else believed in their books. She has built campaigns for faith based fiction, middle grade adventure, sweeping romance, and heartfelt womens fiction, treating every manuscript like it deserves a real spotlight rather than a form letter.

Her approach blends genuine press relationships, sharp content, and a real love of reading, so every campaign feels custom built for that specific book. Authors come to Sheila when they want more than a press release. They want someone who reads the whole manuscript, understands exactly who the audience is, and fights to put the book in front of readers who will love it.

Genre Focus: Womens Fiction And Healing Stories

Working across such different worlds keeps me sharp. Redeemed asked me to honor a biblical story with the reverence it deserved. Game On Boys asked me to think like a ten year old gamer and get a book into the hands of reluctant readers. Loving The Billionaire Army Doc asked for pure swoon and momentum. Life After You asked me to sit with grief and healing and still leave room for hope. Every one of these books taught me something new about the reader on the other side of the page, and that is exactly why I do this work.
